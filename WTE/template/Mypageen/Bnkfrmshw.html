Bank transfer

If you would like to purchase using your bank account, please send us the information below. *Only spot plans can be purchased.

How to use your points (by bank transfer)

  1. We will inform the transfer account for the person who made the inquiry. Please make transfer by the scheduled date.
  2. We will confirm the payment.It will take 2-3 business days to confirm, including the transfer date.
  3. Points will be granted as soon as payment is completed.
  4. The secretariat will contact you as soon as the points are granted.
  • The transfer fee is at customer’s cost.
  • You cannot cancel the points you purchased, thank you for your understanding in advance.
  • It takes 2-3 business days to confirm the payment, including the transfer date.
