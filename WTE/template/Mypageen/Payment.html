Card payment flow

%product_name% accepts credit card payments or bank payments.
Credit card payments are made using PayPal.

Card payment flow

Click the [Credit card payment] menu.

Select the desired plan and click the [Proceed to confirmation screen] button.

Check if the plan you ordered is correct. If there is no problem, click [Proceed to card payment procedure]

A field will appear for you to enter your credit card information.
Enter your card information and click the [Pay Now] button.

  • Expired credit cards cannot be used
  • It may take some time for the screen to switch, but there is no problem if you click the [Pay now] button once.
When the screen changes as shown below, it is complete.
It may take some time to grant points, so please check your points at the top of My Page.

Cancellation process

Click the [Credit card payment] menu. Click the cancel button that appears.
A confirmation screen asking "Are you sure you want to cancel?" will be displayed, so select "OK".
Cancellation is complete when the message "Cancellation has been completed" appears.
